We, a team of 6 friends, had an idea of making a documentary on garbage when we had a chapter about it in our school. We also had sessions in our school where we discussed this topic.
The main concept why we started this documentary is because the state of the garbage is getting worse day by day. If we leave it the same, one day all the things will go wild.
The concept of making a documentary on Garbage started with Abhishek and Sameer, shot by Surya, scripted by Thanmayee and Sparsha, Maithili shared many creative ideas. Finally edited by Sameer.
We worked collaboratively to make this documentary successful and later published.
